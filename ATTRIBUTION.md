# Attribution & Licences

Blazon renders coats of arms using heraldic charge artwork sourced from
**[DrawShield](https://drawshield.net)** by **Karl Wilcox** — DrawShield's code is
**GPL-3.0**. The charge SVGs are contributed heraldic art, predominantly from
**Wikimedia Commons** (notably **User:Sodacan**) and the **[Viking Answer Lady](http://www.vikinganswerlady.com)**.

These assets are **predominantly licensed CC BY-SA** (a few are public domain or GPL).
Per-file machine-readable licences are often absent upstream, so **we treat the whole
collection as CC BY-SA**: we attribute the creators (below), and **coats of arms generated
by Blazon that incorporate this artwork are likewise made available under CC BY-SA**
(attribution + share-alike). Recoloured charges are derivative works under the same terms.

The cleaned, recoloured SVGs we serve live in `public/charges/`; this file and
`public/charges/attribution.json` are the machine-readable record.
