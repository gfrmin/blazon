# Attribution & Licences

Blazon renders coats of arms using heraldic charge artwork from
**[DrawShield](https://drawshield.net)** by **Karl Wilcox** (code **GPL-3.0**). The charge
SVGs (2108, hosted on Cloudflare R2) are contributed heraldic art, predominantly from
**[Wikimedia Commons](https://commons.wikimedia.org)** (notably **User:Sodacan**) and the
**[Viking Answer Lady](http://www.vikinganswerlady.com)**.

Predominantly **CC BY-SA** (some public domain / GPL); per-file licences are often absent
upstream, so the collection is treated as CC BY-SA — creators are attributed below, and coats
of arms generated by Blazon are likewise shared under CC BY-SA (attribution + share-alike).

Non-heraldic / IP categories (Warhammer, playing-cards, sci-fi, franchise characters, board
games, modern objects, sports, dinosaurs) and non-Western traditions (Japanese mon, Norse) are
excluded. Generated by `scripts/vendor-all-charges.mjs`.
